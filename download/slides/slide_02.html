PROBLEM · UMKM + INVESTOR GAP

Indonesia's MSMEs are massive,
but investors have no access.

64 million MSMEs contribute 60% of Indonesia's GDP, but 9 out of 10 cannot access bank loans. Retail investors also have no small-ticket investment channel to local businesses — minimum Rp 100 million at securities firms, no fractional ownership, no real-time profit.

  • Bank loans require collateral & 6-12 month process — 87% of MSMEs rejected
  • Public stocks require Rp 100M min — barrier too high for Gen Z & millennials
  • Conventional crowdfunding charges platform fee 10-20% & monthly manual transfers
